Local cannabis menu (The Vault)

Cannabis products are not shipped by mail or common carriers. Fulfillment is limited to local pickup and local delivery coordination for verified 21+ customers, with valid government-issued ID required at fulfillment.

  • Reservations: May require phone confirmation; availability rotates.
  • Final sale: Cannabis sales are typically final once fulfilled, subject to applicable law.
