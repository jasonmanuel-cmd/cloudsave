ABOUT CLOUD AVE

BAKERSFIELD · KERN COUNTY · ALL ON DA MUSCLE

Roots

Cloud Ave grew out of Bakersfield: cannabis-first discipline, then apparel as an extension of the same name and energy. The goal has always been direct relationships, real inventory, and zero corporate theater.

Two sides, one brand

Lifestyle is premium tees, hats, beanies, and combos—built to ship across the U.S. The Vault is the 21+ local menu for Kern County: pickup and delivery coordination only, with valid ID at fulfillment. Cannabis is never shipped by mail.

Community

We rep the city that built us. Every drop and every local interaction is meant to feel loud, clean, and personal—whether you’re pulling up in Bakersfield or repping the ave from another state.
