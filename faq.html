FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

General

What is Cloud Ave?
Cloud Ave is a Bakersfield-rooted brand: premium streetwear and accessories that ship nationwide, plus a separate 21+ local menu (The Vault) for Kern County pickup and delivery coordination only.

How do I reach you?
DM Instagram or use the Contact page. A phone number will be posted when it’s set.

Lifestyle (apparel)

Do you ship apparel outside Bakersfield?
Yes. Lifestyle items are built for nationwide shipping where permitted. Checkout and carriers are described at order time.

Can I return or exchange apparel?
Unworn items in original packaging may be eligible for exchange or store credit within a limited window—see Shipping & Returns and confirm with support before sending anything back.

The Vault (local menu)

Do you ship cannabis?
No. Cannabis products are never shipped by mail or common carriers. Fulfillment is local pickup or local delivery coordination for qualified customers, with valid ID at fulfillment.

Who can access The Vault?
You must be 21+ and meet local requirements (e.g., Kern County). ID is verified at fulfillment.

How do I reserve Vault items?
Use the call-to-reserve flow on product cards. Availability rotates—phone confirmation keeps fulfillment accurate.

Compliance

Where are your policies?
Terms, Privacy, and Shipping & Returns cover use of the site, data practices, and fulfillment rules.
